Game Rules:


1. Click on "New Game" to start a new game.

2. Click on "Roll Dice".Then a dice will appear with number in between 1 to 6.

3. If you get 1 then your current score will become 0 and it will be other player's turn.

4. If you get 2 to 6 then it will add the number to your current score till you press "Hold" button.

5. "Hold" button will add your current score to the total score and pass the turn to other player.

6. Player who scores 100 will be the Winner and background will become Grey.
